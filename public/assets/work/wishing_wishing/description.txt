A magical wishing pool - an interactive space for people to connect with their inner desires.
I've always been drawn to mysticism, energy work, and symbolic rituals. These spiritual tools have helped me understand myself better, and stay grounded while becoming a healthier, stronger version of who I am.
Now, I want to share that energy with others. Through this project, I hope to offer a healing and reflective space where people can make heartfelt wishes, explore their own energy, and feel supported - like a gentle blessing from the universe, passed through a star little pool.
